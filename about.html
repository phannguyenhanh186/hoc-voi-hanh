About

hoc voi hanh ("learning goes hand in hand with practice") is a place to learn Vietnamese through real conversations.

Each lesson has a tap-each-word dialogue (with audio and toggleable English meanings), short grammar points, a vocabulary list, and interactive practice (matching, reordering, dictation, gap filling, and translating).

You can learn with a Southern or Northern accent, and progress level by level: mầm → chồi → lá.
