Your Roadmap to Fluency

We track progress through Hours of Immersion rather than months or years. The reasoning is straightforward: claiming "I've studied for 5 years" doesn't mean much if those years only added up to 10 minutes a week. Hours actually reflect real exposure, which is why they're the metric that matters.

The approach is simple. You begin by listening and shadowing phrases that native speakers use on a daily basis.

The more hours you accumulate, the more fluent and complex your speech becomes, gradually moving you closer to sounding like a native speaker.
